Brand guidelines

SillyLittleTech

Non-profit open-source project. We kindly ask you get our permission before using our logo — see the policy below.

Typography

Primary display font (example):

Fallback usage:

Body copy should remain readable and accessible. Use line-height ~1.5 and maintain contrast.

Logo usage & policy

We are a not-for-profit project. Only affiliated projects should use our logo with our permission. If you wish to use the logo for marketing, please contact us at the project email listed in our repo.

  • Do not modify the logo mark or colors.
  • Always provide adequate clear space around the mark.
  • Prefer the raster PNG provided in assetlab/raster for web use.

Badges (raster)
